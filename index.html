5 Tips to Stay Fit

1. Exercise at least 30 minutes daily.
2. Eat balanced meals with protein and veggies.
3. Stay hydrated throughout the day.
4. Sleep at least 7-8 hours every night.
5. Keep a positive mindset and stay consistent.

Healthy Eating Habits

Focus on natural, unprocessed foods. Avoid sugar drinks and fried foods. Eat fruits and drink plenty of water daily.
